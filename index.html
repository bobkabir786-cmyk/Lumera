Our Story

Timeless Glow. Effortless Beauty.

Born in the lush, serene landscapes of Guwahati, Assam, Lumera was founded with a singular, unwavering goal: to harmonize the pristine purity of nature with advanced, clinical skincare science. Inspired by the rich biodiversity of the Northeast and the restorative power of ocean-mineral hydration, our formulations are meticulously crafted to repair, protect, and deeply nourish. We believe that true beauty shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to provide you with sophisticated, clean essentials that honor your skin's natural balance, unveiling a radiant complexion that lasts.

The Science of Lumera

Where clinical dermatology meets botanical synergy.

Bio-Active Botanical Synergy

We extract highly concentrated, resilient antioxidants from the indigenous flora of Assam, sourcing pure plant complexes that naturally target cellular repair and environment-induced stress defense.

Oceanic Micro-Hydration

Utilizing high-affinity marine macrominerals, our formulas closely mirror the skin’s native structural components. This promotes optimized cellular water absorption down through the epidermal matrices.

Clinical Delivery Systems

Pure ingredients require intelligent pathways. Our bio-engineered delivery agents encapsulate heavy actives like Niacinamide and Vitamin C, preventing surface oxidation for maximum epidermal stability.
